About Us:

Jay Landi is a remarkable individual who possesses an insatiable thirst for knowledge and an unwavering passion for intellectual pursuits. As a freshman in middle school, he has already delved into the intricate worlds of Japanese language and quantum physics, displaying a level of curiosity and dedication beyond his years. What sets Jay apart is not only his impressive academic endeavors, but also his unique physical attribute - an absolutely massive cranium that, astonishingly, accounted for a staggering 95% of his birth weight. This intriguing aspect of his anatomy has fueled Jay's desire to explore the realms of neuroscience and cognitive studies, hoping to unravel the mysteries of the human mind and its infinite potential.

Carina Guo, on the other hand, brings her own exceptional qualities to the table, proving that age is no barrier when it comes to making a meaningful impact. Despite being a senior in elementary school, Carina possesses an uncanny aptitude for problem-solving and a profound interest in diverse fields. Currently, she is immersing herself in the art of Sudoku, mastering its intricate puzzles with an unmatched zeal for mental challenges. Additionally, Carina has embarked on a unique educational journey in biomoleculechemigineering, a complex discipline that merges the principles of biology, chemistry, and engineering. Her pursuit of this subject stems from a deeply personal reason - an undeniable and, at times, horrific eyesight since childhood. This firsthand experience has ignited Carina's determination to explore the fascinating world of ophthalmology and develop innovative solutions to improve vision and enhance the lives of individuals facing similar challenges.

Jay and Carina's paths crossed serendipitously, driven by a shared vision and a desire to create something extraordinary. Together, they founded eyePod, an innovative venture that seamlessly blends their diverse interests and complementary skills. With Jay's intellectual prowess and Carina's tenacity, they aim to revolutionize the way we perceive the world and interact with technology. Through their collaboration, eyePod endeavors to develop cutting-edge advancements in neurobiology, artificial intelligence, and human-computer interfaces, all with the ultimate goal of enhancing the human experience and pushing the boundaries of scientific understanding.

As Jay and Carina embark on this exhilarating journey, they are fueled by a collective determination to make a lasting impact on society. Their story serves as a powerful reminder that age and physical attributes should never limit one's aspirations. Instead, it is the unwavering passion, relentless pursuit of knowledge, and the audacity to dream big that truly define individuals like Jay and Carina. Together, they are poised to reshape the world we live in, leaving an indelible mark on the realms of science, innovation, and human potential.
